Detroit counts on tech to prep for the 2020 Census

FEB. 6, GOVTECH.COM ↗

The city of Detroit estimates that upwards of 27,000 households did not partake in the 2010 census, depriving the city of crucial funding opportunities. For 2020, Detroit plans to take a reinvented approach, one that unites data and technology.

Effects of this story

  • Grants Detroit a bigger slice of $675 billion in federal funding
  • Could earn another congressional seat
  • Plans to promote positive relationships throughout neighborhoods
  • Gives the state/country a better understanding of Detroit
  • Will likely develop an application(s) to improve Detroit for its citizens
  • Requires all residents of Detroit to have an internet-connected smart device


Who's affected?
All residents of Detroit (postively), city officials (positively)
