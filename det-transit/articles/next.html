Our Directions

Josh Krauth-Harding ’s direction will focus on the safety of residents (primarily young women) when using Detroit’s public transit system. It will look to successful awareness campaigns, phone applications, and video pieces to produce an outcome that will serve Detroiters and assure their safety when traveling.

Tracee Hartley is planning on creating an awareness campaign to help highlight the flaws inside the Detroit public transportation system. She will be designing to reach the people of power within the mobility authority. She plans to do this through forms of experiential art, print, and social media campaigning. Her objective is to influence Detroit to question the decisions of the Detroit mobility authorities.
